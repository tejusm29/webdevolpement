Classroom Foundational Course

The Classroom Foundation Course has a curriculum that is designed with a thoughtful mix of knowledge and application. The UPSC exam focuses on the capacity of an aspirant to apply the knowledge they learn. and the course aims to impart this lesson to its students over several months. The student is tested on their knowledge for both Preliminary and Mains examination through the rigorous testing schedule. Every week, the students are expected to write the weekly test. After which, a live test analysis highlights relevant information and fills any gap in the student’s knowledge. The faculty goes above and beyond to ensure that the students are well equipped with the knowledge and skills to crack the UPSC exam.

Full Course

The full course offers you a comprehensive, all-around development opportunity. It covers the complete range from Preliminary to Mains preparation.
